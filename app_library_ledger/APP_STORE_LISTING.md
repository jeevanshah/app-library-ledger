### Description
Tired of having 20+ similar apps doing the same thing? 

App Library Ledger helps you take control of your app chaos.

**Catalog all your apps** — Add them in seconds with name, App Store link, and purpose.

**Tag by purpose** — Use 10 pre-built categories (Productivity, Notes, Finance, Health, Media, Utilities, Social, Education, Shopping, Travel) or create your own.

**Spot duplicates instantly** — See at a glance how many apps are in each category. Find redundancy and declutter your home screen.

**100% Private** — Offline-only app. All data stays on your device. No accounts, no tracking, no ads.

Perfect for:
- App hoarders (you know who you are)
- Productivity enthusiasts
- Students organizing their study apps
- Anyone with too many apps

**Features:**
✅ Offline-first (100% private)  
✅ Quick add (10 seconds per app)  
✅ Custom categories  
✅ Search & filter  
✅ No account needed  
✅ Works on iPhone & iPad  

Stop wondering what app does what. Organize once, thank yourself forever.
